1. PROCEDURE AND POLICY DOCUMENTATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Vulnerability Management Procedure (VMP-UK-2025 v2.4, approved 22 October
2025 by CISO delegate A. Nwosu, Head of Cyber Defence) defines the programme for
identifying, assessing, prioritising, and remediating technical vulnerabilities
across all Vodafone UK in-scope infrastructure and applications. The document is
version-controlled in the corporate document management system (IS-PROC-0078) and
was last reviewed on 22 October 2025 with sign-off by Information Asset Owner
D. Ramirez (Director of Infrastructure). Remediation SLAs are codified in
Section 5 of the procedure:

  - Critical (CVSS >= 9.0):  14 calendar days
  - High (CVSS 7.0 -- 8.9):  30 calendar days
  - Medium (CVSS 4.0 -- 6.9): 90 calendar days
  - Low (CVSS < 4.0):         Next scheduled maintenance window

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. VULNERABILITY SCAN SUMMARY -- Q1 2026
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Authenticated scans are executed using Tenable.io (infrastructure) and
DAST/SAST tooling integrated into the CI/CD pipeline (applications). Scan
results feed into the Vodafone UK Vulnerability Tracking Register (VTR),
maintained in ServiceNow.

Infrastructure Scan Coverage (monthly cadence):
  - Scan dates Q1 2026: 08-Jan, 06-Feb, 06-Mar
  - Total assets in scope: 3,847 (servers, network devices, endpoints)

    report exported 08 Apr 2026)
  - Total unique vulnerabilities identified Q1 2026: 1,284
    - Critical: 23
    - High:    187
    - Medium:  641
    - Low:     433

Application Scan Coverage (quarterly cadence):
  - Q1 2026 scan completed: 14 -- 17 March 2026 (29 applications in scope)
  - Applications scanned: 29 / 29 (100%)
  - Vulnerabilities identified from application scans: 94 (High: 12, Medium: 82)
