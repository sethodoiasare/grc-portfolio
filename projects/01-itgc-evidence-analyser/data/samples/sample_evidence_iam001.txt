1. PROCEDURE DOCUMENTATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The User Access Management Procedure (UAMP-UK-2025 v3.2, approved 14 November
2025 by CISO delegate J. Hartley, Director of Identity and Access Management)
defines the end-to-end lifecycle for user account creation, modification, and
de-registration across all Vodafone UK in-scope production systems. The procedure
is published on the Vodafone UK InfoSec SharePoint intranet (document ID
IS-PROC-0042) and version-controlled within the corporate document management
system. The most recent annual review was completed on 14 November 2025, with
documented sign-off from the Information Asset Owner (IAO), P. Okafor (VP
Technology Platforms). The previous version (v3.1, approved November 2024) was
superseded following the migration to the ServiceNow ITSM platform in Q3 2025.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. ITSM TICKET WORKFLOW -- USER REGISTRATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

All new user account requests are submitted via ServiceNow (instance:
vodafone-uk.service-now.com) using the "New User Access Request" (NUAR) catalogue
item. The workflow enforces the following mandatory fields before submission:
requester employee ID, new user full name, job title, department, cost centre,
line manager approval (captured as a ServiceNow approval task), list of systems
and access levels required, and a free-text business justification field.

The ServiceNow workflow routes each NUAR ticket to the IAO or their delegate for
a secondary approval gate before the Access Management team provisions the
account. Provisioning confirmation is recorded as a resolution note on the ticket
with a timestamp. No account creation is permitted outside of this workflow;
direct provisioning requests via email or verbal instruction are rejected per
UAMP-UK-2025 Section 4.3.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. HR SYSTEM INTEGRATION -- LEAVER PROCESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Vodafone UK's HR system (Workday) is integrated with ServiceNow via a nightly
automated data feed. When a leaver event is recorded in Workday (resignation,
termination, or end of contract), a ServiceNow "Leaver De-registration" (LDR)
ticket is automatically created by 06:00 the following business day. The ticket
triggers a workflow that:

  (a) Suspends all Active Directory accounts associated with the leaver within
      4 hours of ticket creation.
  (b) Revokes VPN and remote access credentials within the same 4-hour window.
  (c) Schedules full account deletion after a 30-day retention period as required
      by the HR data retention policy (HDRET-UK-2024).

During Q1 2026, 47 leaver events were processed. All 47 met the one business day
SLA for account deactivation. The Access Management team conducted a manual
reconciliation of Workday leaver records against ServiceNow LDR tickets on
31 January, 28 February, and 31 March 2026; no discrepancies were identified.
