# About Me

I'm a Cyber Assurance Manager at Vodafone Group UK, where I lead IT infrastructure and cybersecurity audits against one of the most mature control frameworks in telecoms. My career has spanned three continents — from cybersecurity engineering at Huawei in Southern Africa, to building the internal audit function at Speechmatics in Cambridge, to managing assurance for Vodafone's UK infrastructure.

What sets me apart: I don't stop at identifying control gaps. I build the automation that closes them.

Over the past year, I've engineered 13 security tools that span the full GRC lifecycle — AI-powered evidence assessment, automated evidence collection, IAM lifecycle auditing, vulnerability SLA tracking, DevSecOps evidence packaging, multi-cloud posture assessment, policy-as-code enforcement, security metrics computation, data classification, control coverage mapping, risk scoring, vendor assessment, and audit readiness dashboards.

Every tool maps findings to real control frameworks. Every tool works in production or demo mode. Every tool is tested and documented.

I hold CISM, CRISC, SecurityX, ISO 27001 Internal Auditor, Pentest+, CySA+, and Azure Security Engineer certifications. I'm completing an MSc in Information Security & Digital Forensics at the University of East London (December 2026).
